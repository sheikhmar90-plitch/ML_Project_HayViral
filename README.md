Overview

This repository contains a collection of Machine Learning projects designed to solve real-world business problems through predictive analytics. The projects focus on risk assessment, employee retention, customer behavior analysis, and sales prediction. By leveraging data-driven insights, these models help organizations make informed decisions, optimize operations, and improve overall performance.

Included Projects
1. Credit Risk Scoring

Predicts the likelihood of loan default based on customer financial and demographic information. This model assists financial institutions in evaluating creditworthiness and minimizing lending risks.

2. HR Attrition Prediction

Analyzes employee-related factors to predict workforce attrition. The model helps organizations identify employees at risk of leaving and develop effective retention strategies.

3. Customer Churn Prediction

Predicts whether customers are likely to discontinue a service or subscription. The project enables businesses to improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

4. Sales Forecasting

Forecasts future sales trends using historical sales data. The model supports inventory planning, budgeting, and strategic business decisions.
